Interpreting Services

Language Barrier is a leading provider of language interpreting services in the United Kingdom. Our team specialises in all types of interpretation, including event, court, conference, video & remote, as well as simultaneous and consecutive. We cover more than 50+ languages and work with clients in any industry. With more than 12 years of expertise in providing interpreting services, our solutions cover:

Consecutive Interpreting

Consecutive Interpreting is the most popular type of interpreting as it does not require any specialist equipment or complex planning. It is very often used in Court hearings, Police and Probation work, Health, Legal and Commercial meetings.

Telephone Interpreting

It is simply Consecutive Interpreting over the phone instead of face to face. It is the ideal solution when geographically dispersed people need to talk but do not share a common language. With help from our interpreters, people are able to communicate by telephone.

Simultaneous Interpreting

Simultaneous interpreting is highly skilled work. As simultaneous interpreters work in real time there are no second chances, they must get it right the first time. Simultaneous interpreters with high levels of expertise and ample experience are vital in making your event a success. All Simultaneous Interpreters supplied by InterpretingDirect have a minimum of 5 years experience and many have in-depth knowledge in their chosen field.
